Brief Bio

Mrs. Ayadi Esther is a passionate and dedicated English Language teacher with over 8 years of experience teaching secondary school students in Lagos. She holds a Bachelor's degree in English Language and a Post-Graduate Diploma in Education. Mrs. Ayadi Esther is committed to inspiring and empowering her students to become effective communicators and critical thinkers.

Specializations:

  • Classroom Management and Instructional Strategies

Philosophy:

Philosophy Overview:

Esther's educational Philosophy centers on the belief that language is both a tool for expression and a gateaway to understanding the world. She views English not merely as a subject, but as a means of empowering students to find their voice, understand others, and engage meaningfully with society.

Client-Centric Approach:

Her student-centric approach emphasizes:
  • Active Learning - fostering inquiry-based discussions, debates, and collaborative projects that encourage deeper engagement with texts and ideas.
  • Empathy and Inclusion - creationg a supportive classroom environment where all learners feel valued, heard, and inspired to grow.
  • Holistic Development - integrating social-emotional learning with language instruction to nurture confident, reflective, and articulate individuals.
